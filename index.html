Faith [Shahadah]

Faith, represented by Shahadah, is the declaration of belief in the oneness of Allah and the prophethood of Muhammad (peace be upon him). It is the foundation of a Muslim's life, guiding actions, thoughts, and intentions.

Prayer [Salah]

Prayer, known as Salah, is the daily act of worship performed five times a day. It serves as a direct connection between a Muslim and Allah, fostering discipline, mindfulness, and spiritual growth.

Charity [Zakat]

Zakat, or almsgiving, is a fundamental pillar of Islam. It involves giving a portion of one's wealth to those in need, fostering social equality and compassion within the community.

Fasting [Sawm]

Pilgrimage [Hajj]

Hajj, the pilgrimage to Mecca, is a once-in-a-lifetime obligation for those who are physically and financially able. It represents unity, equality, and devotion to Allah, bringing Muslims from all over the world together in worship.
